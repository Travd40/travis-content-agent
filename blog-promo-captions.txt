"Why does my husband shut down?"

If you've Googled that, here's what I'd tell you
as someone who was the silent man for 15 years.

It's almost never about you.

When a man goes quiet, he's running this calculation:
→ Will my point land, or get turned around on me?
→ Will I get to finish, or get interrupted?
→ Will this end better than where it started?

If the answer's no — he tunes out. Not as punishment.
As protection.

Silence isn't always peace. Sometimes it's a wall
you didn't know was being built.

WEEK 2 — "Communication Isn't a Courtroom"
URL: https://travisdixoncoaching.com/blog/week-2-communication-isnt-a-courtroom
----------------------------------------------------------------

Most couples don't have a communication problem.
They have a courtroom problem.

Every disagreement turns into:
→ Who's right
→ Who started it
→ Who has more receipts

That's not a conversation. That's a trial.
And trials don't build relationships — they end them.

Stop arguing like a lawyer. Start showing up like a teammate.

URL: https://travisdixoncoaching.com/blog/week-4-the-50-50-marriage-myth

50/50 marriages don't work.

The math sounds fair. The math kills relationships.

Because some weeks she's at 30%. Some weeks
he's at 30%. If you're keeping score, the moment
one person drops below 50, the other one stops giving 100.

Real partnership is 100/100.
You both bring everything you have. Every time.
